input_img ...50jpeg images (filename; No/ No=01~50)
input_mask ...50 bmp images (filename; No_M)
output ...50bmp images (filename; No_after_4)


01_M.bmp is the mask image for 01.jpg,
and 01_after_4.bmp is the output images from 01.jpg & 01_M.bmp.


There are 50 images in the input_img directory,
but acctually there are only 40 different kind of images.

For example, 41.jpg is same as 01.jpg,
but 41_M.bmp and 01_M.bmp are different,
so 41_after_4.bmp and 01_after_4.bmp are different too.
